F.A.Q.

Is Ace safe to use?

Just like the LCU is in beta, so is Ace. We have tried to make Ace as error-proof as possible, but you might encounter issues we haven't even thought about. From our experiences however, Ace is just as safe as the normal client.

Can Ace implement a plugin for X?

Maybe. Just hit us up at our Discord or the Github Repository, and we will see what we can do.

Ace isn't working! What to do?

If you encounter any issues with the launcher or Ace itself, don't hesitate to let us know! Either file an issue at our Github Repository, or hit us up at #bugs in our Discord.
